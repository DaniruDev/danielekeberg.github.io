Group Tickets: Explore SCience Together!

Looking for an engaging and educational experience for your group? Look no further than our interactive science museum! Whether you’re a school group, a community organization, or simply a bunch of friends with a shared curiosity for the wonders of science, we’ve got you covered with our group ticket packages.

How to Book Group Tickets:

Contact Us: Reach out to our friendly group sales team to discuss your group’s needs, preferences and budget. We’ll work with you to tailor a customized ticket package that suits your requirements and ensures a seamless booking process

Choose Your Date: Select your preferred visit date and time from our available options. Whether you’re planning a weekday excursion or a weekend adventure, we’ll do our best to accommodate your group’s schedule and ensure a hassle-free experience from start to finish.

Secure Your Spot: Once you’ve finalized your booking details, simply make your payment to secure your group’s spot at our museum. Rest assured, our flexible booking policies and dedicated support team are here to assist you every step of the way.

Get Ready to Explore: On the day of your visit, arrive with your group ready to embark on an unforgettable journey through the wonders of science. Our knowledgeable staff will be on hand to welcome you, provide any necessary information, and ensure you have everything you need for a fantastic day of exploration.

Ready to spark curiosity, inspire discovery, and ignite imaginations with your group? Book your group tickets today and get ready for an adventure like no other at our interactive science museum!
